Lisbon is the capital city of Portugal located in western Portugal on the estuary of the Tagus River. History, Lisbon was founded by Phoenicians and they established a settlement called Ulissipo, which was then conquered by the Greeks Carthaginians. Facts; #1 Lisbon was completely destroyed in the earthquake of 1755, #2 In Lisbon, you are literally walking on the carpet of stone, #3 There is an entire museum dedicated to Azulejos. Attractions; #1 Castelo de Sao Jorge:An Iconic Landmark, #2 Mosterio dos Jerominos: Built in Honor of Portugal's Age of Discovery and #3 Oceanario de Lisboa: A Modern Aquarium.
